Objective

  1. To save from sin and guide into service. The enemy will not prevail against youth who are actively engaged in the things of God.

  2. “He [Satan] well knows that there is no other class that can do as much good as young men and young women who are consecrated to God. The youth, if right, could sway a mighty influence. Preachers or laymen advanced in years, cannot have one-half the influence upon the young that the youth, devoted to God, can have upon their associates” (Messages to Young People, p. 204).

  3. “Young men and women, God calls upon you to work, work for Him… You can do a work that those who minister in word and doctrine cannot do. You can reach a class whom the minister cannot affect” (Messages to Young People, p. 207).
